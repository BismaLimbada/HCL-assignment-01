Phase 2: Performance Prediction (GOMS)

A decomposition of expert tasks into Goals, Operators, Methods, and Selection rules.

[Image of GOMS model structure showing Goals, Operators, Methods, and Selection Rules]
GOALS
High-level objectives such as SET-DOSAGE or SILENCE-ALARM.
OPERATORS
The most basic motor or cognitive acts like PRESS-KEY or READ-SCREEN.
METHODS
Learned procedures or sequences of operators used to accomplish a goal.
SELECTION RULES
The logic used to choose a specific method when multiple options are available.
